About the Learning Point

The Learning Point at MMU Cyberjaya is a dedicated space for student learning and collaboration. It provides access to a range of resources and services to support academic success and personal development.

Key Features

  • Study areas with comfortable seating
  • Computer workstations for research and assignments
  • Access to online resources and databases
  • Printing and photocopying facilities
  • Assistance from student mentors

Location

The Learning Point is centrally located within the MMU Cyberjaya campus, making it easily accessible for students.
