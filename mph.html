MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the MPH

The Multipurpose Hall (MPH) at MMU Cyberjaya is a versatile venue used for various events and activities. It serves as a space for university ceremonies, conferences, seminars, workshops, and student events.

Key Features

  • Spacious hall with seating capacity for large audiences
  • Stage area for performances, presentations, and ceremonies
  • Audiovisual equipment for multimedia presentations
  • Flexible seating arrangements for different event formats
  • Adjacent facilities for catering and refreshments

Location

The MPH is located at a central location within the MMU Cyberjaya campus, making it easily accessible for students and faculty members.
