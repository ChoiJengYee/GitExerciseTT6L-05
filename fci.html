Faculty of Computing and Informatics (FCI) Building

MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the FCI Building

The FCI Building at MMU Cyberjaya is a modern facility dedicated to computing and informatics studies. It houses advanced computer labs, research centers, and multimedia facilities, providing students with a dynamic learning environment.

Key Features

  • Advanced computer labs
  • Research centers
  • Multimedia facilities for creative projects
  • Collaborative spaces for group projects
  • Faculty offices and meeting rooms
  • Study rooms for students

Location

The FCI Building is located at the heart (middle) of the MMU Cyberjaya campus, easily accessible from various parts of the university.
