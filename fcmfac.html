Faculty of Creative Multimedia (FCM) Building

MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the FCM Building

The FCM Building at MMU Cyberjaya is a vibrant space for creative multimedia education. It features studios, editing suites, and exhibition spaces, providing students with a dynamic environment for exploring various forms of digital media, where the third floor of the FCM building is the Faculty of Applied Communication (FAC).

Key Features

  • Art studios for animation and visual effects
  • Editing suites for post-production work
  • Exhibition spaces for showcasing student projects
  • Collaborative areas for creative collaboration
  • Faculty offices and meeting rooms
  • Study rooms for students

Location

The FCM Building is centrally located within the MMU Cyberjaya campus, providing easy access for students and faculty members.
