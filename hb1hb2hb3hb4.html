About HB1, HB2, HB3, and HB4 Buildings

The HB1 to HB4 buildings at MMU Cyberjaya are the accommodation for students,it has various administrative functions and facilities.

Key Features

  • Rooms with tables,chairs, beds, cupboards and fans
  • Toilets for students
  • Study areas for students
  • Accessible location within the campus
  • Parking areas
  • Food stalls for students' convinient

Location

The HB1 to HB4 buildings are located in the central area of the MMU Cyberjaya campus, making them easily accessible for students and faculty members.
