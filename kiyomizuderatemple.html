Kiyomizu-dera Temple

Kiyomizu-dera is an independent Buddhist temple in eastern Kyoto, Japan. The temple is part of the Historic Monuments of Ancient Kyoto UNESCO World Heritage site.

The temple is known for its wooden stage that juts out from its main hall, 13 meters above the hillside below. The stage affords visitors a nice view of the cherry and maple trees below that erupt in a sea of color in spring and fall, as well as of the city of Kyoto in the distance.
