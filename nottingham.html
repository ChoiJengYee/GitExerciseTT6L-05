Nottingham

Nottingham is a city in the East Midlands region of England. It is known for its role in the Robin Hood legend and for its historic lace-making and bicycle industries.

The city is home to Nottingham Castle, a medieval castle now housing an art gallery and museum; the Old Market Square, the largest public square in the UK; and the Lace Market, a historic area containing many of the city's best-preserved Georgian buildings.

Nottingham is also known for its vibrant nightlife, with many bars, pubs, and clubs located in the city center and the Hockley area. The city is also home to two universities, the University of Nottingham and Nottingham Trent University.
