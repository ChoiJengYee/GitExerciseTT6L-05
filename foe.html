Faculty of Engineering (FOE) Building

MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the FOE Building

The FOE Building at MMU Cyberjaya is a modern facility equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, workshops, and classrooms. It is home to various engineering departments, providing students with a dynamic learning environment.

Key Features

  • Advanced laboratories for practical experiments
  • Workshops for engineering projects
  • Classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids
  • Faculty offices and meeting rooms
  • Collaborative spaces for group projects
  • Study rooms for students

Location

The FOE Building is located in the engineering precinct of the MMU Cyberjaya campus, easily accessible from various parts of the university.
