Faculty of Management (FOM) Building

MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the FOM Building

The FOM Building at MMU Cyberjaya is a modern facility designed for management education and research. It features lecture halls, seminar rooms, and a business library, providing students with a conducive environment for learning.

Key Features

  • Lecture halls equipped with modern teaching aids
  • Seminar rooms for group discussions and presentations
  • Business library with a wide range of resources
  • Faculty offices and meeting rooms
  • Collaborative spaces for group projects

Location

The FOM Building is centrally located within the MMU Cyberjaya campus, making it easily accessible for students and faculty members.
