About the MMU Stadium

The MMU Stadium at MMU Cyberjaya is a modern facility that provides students to do sports whenever.

Key Features

  • Main playing field suitable for various sports
  • Seating capacity for thousands of spectators
  • Restrooms
  • Locker rooms and facilities for athletes
  • Press boxes and media facilities for coverage of events
  • Parking facilities for attendees
