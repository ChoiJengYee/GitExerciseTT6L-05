About the Siti Hasmah Digital Library

The Siti Hasmah Digital Library at MMU Cyberjaya is a modern facility that provides access to a vast collection of digital resources. It offers a wide range of online databases, e-books, and e-journals, enhancing the research and learning experience for students and faculty members.

Key Features

  • Access to online databases and e-resources
  • E-books and e-journals on various subjects
  • Computer terminals for research and study
  • Quiet study areas for individual work
  • Assistance from library staff for research inquiries

Location

The Siti Hasmah Digital Library is located at the heart of the MMU Cyberjaya campus, providing easy access for students and faculty members.
