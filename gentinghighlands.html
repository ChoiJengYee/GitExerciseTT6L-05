Genting Highlands

Genting Highlands is a mountain resort in Malaysia located near the border with Selangor. It is known for its cool climate, theme parks, and casinos.

The resort was founded in 1965 by Lim Goh Tong and has since grown into a popular destination for both locals and tourists. It is accessible by road and cable car, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding rainforest.

Attractions in Genting Highlands include the Genting Skyway, an indoor theme park, outdoor activities like golf and horse riding, as well as shopping and dining options.
