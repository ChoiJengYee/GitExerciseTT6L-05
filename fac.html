Faculty of Applied Communication (FAC)

MMU Cyberjaya Campus

About the FAC

The FAC students are taught on the development of creativity, innovation, analytical and practical knowledge as well as skills directed towards strategic applications of communication.

Key Features

  • Art studios for animation and visual effects
  • Editing suites for post-production work
  • Exhibition spaces for showcasing student projects
  • Collaborative areas for creative collaboration
  • Faculty offices and meeting rooms
  • Study rooms for students

Location

The FAC is located within the FCM Building, providing easy access for students and faculty members.
